### **1. What is a VPN?**
A VPN encrypts your internet traffic and hides your real IP address.

### **2. How does a VPN protect me?**
It prevents websites, hackers, and ISPs from tracking your true identity.

### **3. Difference between VPN and Proxy?**
VPN = IP hiding + encryption  
Proxy = IP hiding only

### **4. Does VPN guarantee full anonymity?**
No. It improves privacy but does not make you fully anonymous.
